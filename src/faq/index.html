Frequently Asked Questions

This doesn't work well on mobile. Why is that?

You're absolutely right. This is the first prototype of the project, so we didn't focus on the mobile experience yet. As we get more free time, we'll get to that as well.

Can I actually schedule a meeting with the folks listed in the image selector?

No. At least not through MeetingShot. Maybe you are good friends with some of them, and if that's the case - you should reach out to them and ask.

Can I style this to look like {my favorite video call app}?

No. We only used the Zoom format as a fun experiment. The project is open source and you can try forking and modifying it to your needs.

Are my custom or webcam images uploaded anywhere?

No. Everything that you see is rendered directly in your browser. We do not store, analyze or send your images anywhere. We are using html2canvas to generate the final image and that's also done directly in your browser and not on some remote server.
