MeetingShot

MeetingShot is a Faux Screenshot Generator designed to help those suffering from FOMMO - Fear of Missing Meetings Online.

Create a Meeting


Created by Dan Fernandez and Den Delimarsky

Get started easily

Select your meeting size between 1 and 9 attendees and click on each attendee to customize their image.

Custom image and webcam support

You can upload custom images (no data is kept on the server) or use capture a realtime camera view of your webcam.

Have Fun

Unleash your creativity adding your favorite celebrities and characters or by creating themed meetings like the "Brady Bunch", "The Karens", or the "Worst.Meeting.Ever".
