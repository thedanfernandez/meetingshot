MeetingShot

MeetingShot is a faux meeting screenshot generator designed to help those suffering from FOMMO - Fear of Missing Meetings Online.

Create a Meeting


Created by Dan Fernandez and Den Delimarsky

Get started easily

Meet with anyone, from one attendee (just yourself), to a group of nine (wow, you must be popular) - in seconds. Zero cost, no encryption, and no support for interactive chat. This is going to be the most fun and most productive meeting you've ever had in your life!

Custom image and webcam support

Don't have any celebrity friends? Don't worry - we don't either. But you can pretend that you are in a meeting with whoever you want. And if you don't find the attendee you want in our (limited) library, you can always upload a custom image or use your own webcam to include the one true celebrity in the meeting.

Have Fun

Unleash your creativity adding your favorite celebrities and characters or by creating themed meetings like "The Brady Bunch", "SNL", or the "Worst. Meeting. Ever." We'll leave it up to you to create your own combinations.
